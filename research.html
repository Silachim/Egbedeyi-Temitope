Current Projects

Temitope has been part of National and International grant-funded research:

  • Eye‑tracking study of Grade 3 multiplicative reasoning.
  • Preservice teachers' embodied noticing during fraction tasks.
  • Place‑based mathematics curriculum grounded in Nigerian contexts.
  • Sociomathematical norms and classroom algorithm choices.
  • Common errors among fourth, fifth, and sixth grade students.
