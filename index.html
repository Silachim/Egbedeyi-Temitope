Welcome

I study how young children build mathematical understanding — with a focus on fraction division, multiplicative reasoning, and place-based approaches connecting classroom learning to community life in Nigeria and the United States.

About Me

I am an early childhood mathematics education researcher whose work investigates students’ multiplicative reasoning, fraction division, sociomathematical norms, and eye-tracking analysis of early mathematical cognition. My scholarship bridges Nigeria and the United States with place-based approaches.

Affiliations: Kent State University
