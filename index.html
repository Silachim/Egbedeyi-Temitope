About Me

I am a mathematics education researcher whose work investigates students’ multiplicative reasoning, fraction division, sociomathematical norms, and eye-tracking analysis of early mathematical cognition. My scholarship focuses on bridging the classroom realities of Nigeria and the United States, emphasizing culturally grounded, place-based mathematics instruction.

Affiliations: Kent State University; The Sanguine Academy.

Personal: Married to Omowumi C. Tope‑Egbedeyi.
