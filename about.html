About Me

Scholar • Researcher • Innovator in Mathematics Education

Temitope F. Egbedeyi is an early childhood mathematics education researcher whose work intersects early childhood mathematics learning, place-based pedagogy, and sociocultural approaches to teacher development. His scholarship spans Nigeria and the United States, emphasizing equitable and community-driven approaches to teaching mathematics.
